# Production Quality Gates

Use this reference to decide whether an episode or short sequence is commercially ready. The goal is to protect retention, payment trust, and drafting clarity.

## Gate Result

Use:

- `green`: ready for draft/serialization.
- `yellow`: usable but needs targeted revision.
- `red`: rebuild before drafting or publishing.

## Single Episode Gate

| Gate | Pass Condition | Red Flag |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Episode job | one clear commercial function | "setup" or "explanation" only |
| Opening pressure | reader understands why this scene matters | slow neutral opening |
| Protagonist action | protagonist causes or changes an event | protagonist only observes |
| Reward | visible payoff appears | no concrete result |
| Situation change | ending state differs from opening state | pieces only move |
| Hook object | ending has concrete object/action/line | vague mood |
| Next-click question | one sentence with specific stake | only "what happens next?" |
| Comment trigger | reaction target exists | nothing to discuss |

## First Episode Gate

1화 must pass:

- genre visible within the first scene or strongly within the first episode
- protagonist pressure or abnormality visible early
- protagonist makes at least one choice
- episode gives first promise of reward
- ending points to a concrete 2화 scene

Red if:

- world history appears before pressure
- many names appear before desire
- protagonist only witnesses the premise
- the ending is abstract narration

## Five Episode Gate

By episode 5:

- the core fun has repeated at least once
- one small reward has paid off
- one external reaction has occurred
- one long-term pressure has appeared
- one comment-worthy moment exists

Red if episodes 2-5 are all explanation, travel, training without proof, or repeated humiliation without counter-movement.

## Paid Conversion Gate

Before paid conversion:

- readers have already received multiple rewards
- the free final episode offers a larger promise
- the first paid episode pays off the free final hook early
- the paid section opens a larger arena without changing genre promise

Red if:

- free final cuts before any reward
- first paid episode begins with recap/explanation
- paid hook is pure bait
- the promised scene is delayed for several paid chapters

## Commercial Scorecard

Score 0-5.

| Category | Question |
| --- | --- |
| Genre fit | Does this episode reward the genre reader? |
| Click pressure | Is the next-click question concrete? |
| Reward trust | Did the reader receive proof that waiting pays? |
| Agency | Did protagonist action create the turn? |
| Comment value | Is there a natural reaction target? |
| Draft clarity | Can a writer draft from this plan? |
| Paid fairness | If paid-adjacent, is the payoff fair? |

Judgment:

- 28+ green
- 21-27 yellow
- 20 or below red

If paid fairness is 2 or below at a paid boundary, mark the whole result red even if the total score is high.
